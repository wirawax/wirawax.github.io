My Achievements:


Because of my devotion, honesty, skill, dicipline, loyal, and faithful service, I was awarded 3rd Class Civil Servant's Long Service Medal (Satyalancana Karya Satya for 10 years of service) from the President of the Republic of Indonesia.

Since 2017, I am the only one of MikroTik Certified Academy Trainer in my educational institution. Since the certificate valid for three years, I had to attend two MikroTik trainings for holding the privilege. Because of pandemic, I couldn't attend the trainings between 2020 to 2022. In November 2023, MikroTik gave me the chance to get it back. Through two trainings (MTCNA and MTCRE) and two passed exams, I got the certificate back.

To be allowed to conduct MikroTik Academy in my educational institution, I have to have one advanced MikroTik Certificate. The one I chose was MTCRE or MikroTik Certified Routing Engineer. Through the training and passing the exam, I got my second valid periode of MTCRE.

To be allowed to conduct MikroTik Academy in my educational institution, I have to have the basic skills of MikroTik RouterOs operation. Through the training and passing the exam, I got my second valid periode of MTCNA.

Selected as one of the best teacher national instructors, I had a chance to apply for overseas teacher training. After some selection steps, I was selected to attend the training, and sent to Seoul, Republic of Korea for three weeks training at Seogang College

As a part of teacher training at Seoul was field work practice at Commax Corporation. Commax is a manufacturer that products smart lock system and smart house system since 1979 in Korea. The field work practice covered smart lock system assembly, quality assurance, and marketing strategies.

MikroTik Academy is a program offered by MikroTik, a router manufacturer from Latvia. Being used worldwide, MikroTik RouterOs handling is an essential competence for our graduates. MikroTik Academy is the one and only certification training provided by MikroTik for formal educational instituion. One of the requirements to be a MikroTik Academy for any educational institution is to have one certified academy trainer. In 2017, I attended the training and got the certificiation.

To be allowed to conduct MikroTik Academy in my educational institution, I have to have one advanced and one basic MikroTik Certificate. The MTCNA certification is the basic requirement that I passed the exam in 2017. The advanced one I chose was MTCRE or MikroTik Certified Routing Engineer. Through the training and passing the exam, I got my first valid periode of MTCNA and MTCRE.

In 2016, Anies Baswedan was the Minister of Education of Republic of Indonesia. One of his program was Learner Teacher. After some trainings and exams, I passed as one of the best national instructor of Learner Teacher in Computer and Network Engineering Subject.

In 2011, I began my magister study at Technology and Vocational Education Study Program, Yogyakarta State University. In 2013, I graduated and got cum-laude. Therefore, I was rewarded as one of the best graduate of Technology and Vocational Education Study Program Yogyakarta State University
